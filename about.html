Over Two Decades of Experience

Springhetti Custom Homes is located in the Fox Valley Region of Northeast Wisconsin near Appleton, Wisconsin. Since 1994, Don Springhetti has been building premier homes that offer superior value.

At Springhetti Custom Homes, we strive to surpass your every expectation. We understand the magnitude of the investment you make when building a home, and take pride in being there for you every step of the way. From suggesting exterior finishes to questions that may arise after you’ve settled in; be assured that we will be there to do whatever it takes to make your Springhetti Home a place you can be proud to call home.


"Our promise is not only to build a strong home, but also to build a strong relationship for many years to come."

-Don Springhetti, President of Springhetti Custom Homes

Superior Value

All of our homes exceed Energy Star® certification requirements with the highest levels of craftsmanship employed in all phases of the home construction.

Exceptional Quality

We strive to build a home superior to those on the market at a competitive price. Our simple process is designed to eliminate surprises, hidden costs, and time delays. We can do this because we have established long-lasting relationships with our suppliers and subcontractors.

Custom Design

When you view our gallery of custom homes, you’ll see that each project is designed with the needs and desires of each customer in mind. We welcome your inquiries and can provide references upon request. You will find that Springhetti Homes can make your dream of owning a custom home a reality.

Services

Lot Selection

If you already have a lot selected, we can guide you through the construction loan process. If you have not chosen a location, we can provide you with information on site availability.

Home Design

We carry a full line of home plans by style and size. We also work with several local architects and engineering firms to ensure that your design is tailored to you.

Finish Selection

Once your home plan has been chosen, we will take you through the process of selecting both interior and exterior finishes. We can make recommendations on everything from color schemes to custom cabinetry to compliment your personal taste.

Construction

After the plans and finishes are in place, we will begin the actual construction of your new home. We provide full-service general management, which means we are your main contact and will be on the job site on a daily basis. This keeps the project running simply and smoothly.

Completion

Your full satisfaction is our goal. Long after you receive the keys to your new home, we will continue to be available to assist you should any future questions or concerns arise.
